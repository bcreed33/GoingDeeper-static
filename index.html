Frequently Asked Questions

  • Who can attend Going Deeper Conference?
  • Going Deeper Conference is designed for junior high, high school and college-age students in the relative age range of 12-29.
  • What does the registration fee cover?
  • The registration fee covers your admission to both days of the conference. It does not include meals, transportation or lodging.
  • Do I have to register now, or can I register at the door?
  • You can purchase tickets at the door, but there is a likely chance it will sell out. Registering today guarantees your spot at this years conference.
  • Can you pay for just one session?
  • No, day and session passes are not available. We recommend that you reserve your place now by registering in full for the conference.
  • Can I drop my student off all day?
  • Yes. There is tons to do in the Strip District during lunch and dinner breaks. There are so many amazing shopping and eating options available.

Mattie Montgomery

  •

Mattie Montgomery is the founder and president of Awakening Evangelism; a ministry that provides instruction and support to believers from all around the world who desire to grow in their ability to share the Gospel and advance the Kingdom more fearlessly in their everyday lives.

After first stepping onto the stage of international ministry during his 10-year stint as the vocalist of renowned spirit-filled hardcore band “For Today,” Mattie developed a passion to serve the Body of Christ as an author, speaker, and instructor. It is his joy to train, equip, and inspire believers to join with the Father in this wonderful work He is doing in our time.

Mattie communicates a fresh, challenging message with a prophetic edge, and takes a hard stand against fear and complacency in the Church. He is covered and sent out by Apostle Aaron Smith of the Rock Church of Mobile, AL. He lives in Mobile with his wife Candice, and their two sons, Kai and Owen.

Noah Nickel

  •

Noah Nickel is the lead pastor of King’s Church in Cleveland, OH. He is filled with a drive and a passion to see people come to know the life changing grace that is found in Jesus and to see a city and its communities transformed. With a heart to build a church of people from every age, race, and walk of life, he is focused on being a part of a church that ‘looks like heaven’.

Noah is originally from Milwaukee, WI. He met his wife, Stephanie at school in Oklahoma, got married in 2005 and have been serving in ministry together ever since. Noah, Stephanie, and their daughter Paisley are believing God to make history in the city of Cleveland through King’s Church and for people to know that in Jesus the best is yet to come!

Jason Howard

  •

Jason Howard is the Senior Associate Pastor at Amplify Church and the Lead Pastor of our Pittsburgh City campus. He also provides leadership for the worship team and leads the creative programming process for weekend services. Jason is a graduate of Hillsong International Leadership College in Sydney, Australia, and he earned a Master’s degree from Fuller Theological Seminary.

Joshua Clear

  •

At only 27 years old, Joshua has spent the past nine years in youth ministry/young adults pouring into youth across western Pennsylvania, telling them just how passionately obsessed Jesus is with each and every one of them.

Joshua serves as the youth and college director at Amplify Church Pittsburgh City Campus.He believes that not only does Jesus love youth and young adults, but He actually likes them and genuinely desires to know them.

Joshua presents the gospel like he does life. The way he communicates and instantly connects with people reflects the connection Jesus so desperately longs for with each person. In the same way, Joshua preaches what he practices. He speaks with passion, love, and sensitivity to the Holy Spirit.

The goal of Joshua's life and ministry is to make Jesus famous by merging culture with the truth of Jesus Christ. He has a finger on the pulse of culture, being fully convinced that he has been called to preach to the upcoming generation. He lives by his personal mission statement "wherever my feet may go revival will come forth."

Pastor Lu

  •

James Llewellyn, better known as Pastor Lu, has been in full time ministry for 18 years. He has served as a Pastor of Student Ministry for fourteen of those years at the Murrysville Alliance Church.

Pastor Lu is currently the Lead Pastor at the Greensburg Alliance Church. He is passionate about Jesus and is compassionate for people. He has spoken at events through out the country encouraging people that God loves them right where they are, but refuses to leave them there. He has been married for 18 years to Cindy, and has two children Dylan and Lilly.

Lauren Blair

Lauren Blair is a daughter of the Father, wife to her best friend, Zack, of 11 years, and proud mom of three beautiful little ones. Lauren has a passion to help love and serve orphans in any way possible. Including bringing an orphan into their home, forever.

Lauren is the co-founder of One More Loved Tables, a business that builds unique farmhouse tables to help fund adoptions for families. She also is the co-founder of Hill City Church. A new church plant that will be launching in the fall of 2017 in Robinson, PA. Lauren loves standing and serving along side her husband in pursuit of the dream that God has put in their hearts! She also loves to teach and encourage. After serving in youth and young adult ministry for 15 years, she discovered her biggest focus is to help people become all that God has created them to be.

Nick Carey

  •

Nick Carey knew he was called to be a pastor at the age of 17 and has been preaching the Good News of Jesus ever since. Nick currently serves as the college pastor for "The U At IUP" at Amplify Church's campus in Indiana, PA.

His desire is for IUP to be known as a place where hurting, broken people can find fulfillment and healing in the person of Jesus. He believes that the Church should be a place where everybody can belong - even if they don't believe. He is unabashedly obsessed with the radical grace of Jesus and aims to build people up with the love of God everyday of his life.
